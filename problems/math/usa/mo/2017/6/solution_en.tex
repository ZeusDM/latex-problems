The answer is $\frac{2}{3}$, achieved when $(a,b,c,d)=(2,2,0,0)$ for example.

Note that for $x\geq0$, $x(x+1)(x-2)^2\geq0$ so $\frac{1}{x^3+4}\geq\frac{1}{4}-\frac{x}{12}$. Thus
\begin{align*}
	\cycsum\frac{a}{b^3+4}&\geq\cycsum\frac{a}{4}-\frac{ab}{12}\\
	&=1-\frac{(a+c)(b+d)}{12}\\
	&\geq1-\frac{4}{12}\\
	&=\frac{2}{3}
\end{align*}
as desired.
