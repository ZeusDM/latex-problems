Find all positive integers 
$n$
 such that the following statement holds: Suppose real numbers 
$a_1$, 
$a_2$, 
$\dots$, 
$a_n$, 
$b_1$, 
$b_2$, 
$\dots$, 
$b_n$
 satisfy 
$|a_k|+|b_k|=1$
 for all 
$k=1,\dots,n$. 
 Then there exists 
$\varepsilon_1$, 
$\varepsilon_2$, 
$\dots$, 
$\varepsilon_n$,
each of which is either 
$-1$
 or 
$1$, 
 such that
\[ \left| \sum_{i=1}^n \varepsilon_i a_i \right| + \left| \sum_{i=1}^n \varepsilon_i b_i \right| \le 1. \]
