Find the largest real constant 
$a$
 such that for all 
$n \geq 1$
 and for all real numbers 
$x_0, x_1, ... , x_n$
 satisfying 
$0 = x_0 < x_1 < x_2 < \cdots < x_n$
 we have
\[\frac{1}{x_1-x_0} + \frac{1}{x_2-x_1} + \dots + \frac{1}{x_n-x_{n-1}} \geq a \left( \frac{2}{x_1} + \frac{3}{x_2} + \dots + \frac{n+1}{x_n} \right)\]
