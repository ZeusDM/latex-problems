Let 
$A_1, B_1$
 and 
$C_1$
 be points on sides 
$BC$, 
$CA$
 and 
$AB$
 of an acute triangle 
$ABC$
 respectively, such that 
$AA_1$, 
$BB_1$
 and 
$CC_1$
 are the internal angle bisectors of triangle 
$ABC$. 
 Let 
$I$
 be the incentre of triangle 
$ABC$, 
 and 
$H$
 be the orthocentre of triangle 
$A_1B_1C_1$. 
 Show that 
$$AH + BH + CH \geq AI + BI + CI.$$
