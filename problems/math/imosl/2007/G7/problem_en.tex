Point $ K$ lies on line $ AD$ such that $ AK = 2R$,  and $ D$ separates $ A$ and $ K$. Lines $ DI$ and $ KI$ meet sides $ AC$ and $ BC$ at $ E,F$ respectively. Let $ IE = IF$.

Prove that $ \angle B\leq 3\angle C$.
