Let 
$O$
be the circumcenter of an acute triangle
$ABC$.
 Line 
$OA$
 intersects the altitudes of 
$ABC$
 through 
$B$
 and 
$C$
 at 
$P$
 and 
$Q$,
 respectively. The altitudes meet at 
$H$.
 Prove that the circumcenter of triangle 
$PQH$
 lies on a median of triangle 
$ABC$.
