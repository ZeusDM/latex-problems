Let 
$a_0,a_1,a_2,\ldots$
 be a sequence of integers and 
$b_0,b_1,b_2,\ldots$
 be a sequence of 
positive
 integers such that 
$a_0=0,a_1=1$,
 and
\[
a_{n+1} =
        \begin{cases}
            a_nb_n+a_{n-1} & \text{if $b_{n-1}=1$} \\
            a_nb_n-a_{n-1} & \text{if $b_{n-1} >1$}
        \end{cases}\qquad\text{for }n=1,2,\ldots.
\]
for 
$n=1,2,\ldots.$
 Prove that at least one of the two numbers 
$a_{2017}$
 and 
$a_{2018}$
 must be greater than or equal to 
$2017$.
