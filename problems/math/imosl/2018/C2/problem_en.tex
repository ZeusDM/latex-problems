\playerA{Queenie} and \playerB{Horst} play a game on a $20 \times 20$ chessboard. In the beginning the board is empty. In every turn, \playerB{Horst} places a black knight on an empty square in such a way that his new knight does not attack any previous knights. Then, \playerA{Queenie} places a white queen on an empty square. The game gets finished when somebody cannot move.

Find the maximal positive $K$ such that, regardless of the strategy of \playerA{Queenie}, \playerB{Horst} can put at least $K$ knights on the board.
