$A, B, C, D$ lie on a circle, and $\frac{AZ \cdot EY  \cdot  ZB  \cdot  Y F}{EZ  \cdot  CY  \cdot  ZF  \cdot Y D}= 1$ .
Let $L = ZX \cap DE,M = XY \cap AF,N = Y Z \cap BC$.
Prove that $L,M,N$ are collinear.
